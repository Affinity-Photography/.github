## What is this Affinity Photo Workflow?

The **Affinity Photo Workflow** represents the industry-leading approach to professional photo editing, combining powerful RAW processing capabilities with sophisticated layer-based compositing tools. This repository serves as a centralized resource hub for photographers, retouchers, and digital artists seeking to master **non-destructive editing**, **color grading**, and **image enhancement techniques**. Whether you specialize in portrait photography, landscape imagery, or commercial product shots, understanding the **Affinity Photo Workflow** is essential for delivering high-quality results with professional efficiency.

## Core Features & Capabilities

- **Non-Destructive Editing:** Master adjustment layers, live filters, and blending modes that preserve original image data while enabling unlimited creative exploration within the **Affinity Photo Workflow** environment.
- **Advanced Selection Tools:** Learn to leverage refinement brushes, selection painting, and channel-based masking for precise compositing and targeted adjustments.
- **RAW Processing Excellence:** Explore comprehensive RAW development controls for exposure recovery, white balance correction, and lens profile applications.

## Affinity Photo Optimization Techniques

To ensure efficient editing workflows, focus on persona selection and workspace organization. The Photo Persona provides comprehensive layer-based editing, while the Develop Persona handles RAW conversion tasks. For complex retouching projects, utilizing the Liquify Persona for subtle adjustments and the Export Persona for delivery optimization can dramatically improve turnaround times within the **Affinity Photo Workflow** ecosystem. Custom keyboard shortcuts and macro recordings accelerate repetitive operations.
